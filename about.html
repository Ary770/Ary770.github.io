Ary is a modern Chassidic Jew, Jazz Drummer, and thinker with a thirst for knowledge, self-growth and drive to make a positive impact on his surroundings. He studied Jewish law and mysticism at the Rabbinical College of America and Mayanot Institute of Jewish Studies in Jerusalem and Web Development at Flatiron School. He is a certified Life Coach with experience in business, online marketing, e-commerce, and coding. He's fluent in Spanish (native), English and well versed in Hebrew. Ary has a big passion for nature, surfing, music performance and learning new things about life and the world we live in.
